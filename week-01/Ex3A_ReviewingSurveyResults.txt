The actual text responses can usually be found by clicking “view responses” or by looking at the full data in Excel. Something tricky about analyzing these answers is that people might write different things even if they mean the same idea, which makes it harder to group the responses.

One challenge is that everyone writes their answers in different ways. Some responses are short and others are longer. This can make it difficult to quickly identify patterns or common themes in the answers.
